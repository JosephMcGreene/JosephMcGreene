Chord Suggestions
Let's Roll

The idea to write a full piano concerto originated partially from a yearning to test my skills in large-scale orchestration, and partly because in doing so, I wanted to be able to take part in the end performance. Since the piano is my most proficient instrument, a piano concerto was born.

The recurring melodic content for the first movement was originally an impromptu I performed for a composition recital in college. The melodies from that impromptu continued bashing desperately against the insides of my skull in an attempt to escape, so I simply caught them in the act of doing so and finally wrote them down two years later.
